## 🏛️ 1. Social Problem Analysis

### ⚠️ The Untapped Energy of Crowds
Taiwan's transit hubs (Taipei Main Station, Banqiao Terminal, MRT Stations) see millions of daily commuters. Currently, the **kinetic energy** (動能) generated by these millions of steps is wasted as friction, heat, and structural vibration.

- **The Challenge**: Taiwan faces severe energy transition pressures. While solar/wind are crucial, "Micro-Generation" (微型發電) in urban locations is currently absent from the grid.
- **The Gap**: Lack of high-engagement, visible green energy solutions that teach citizens about carbon intensity in real-time.

### 🎯 Target Demographic
- **Public Sector**: Taipei/Kaohsiung MRT systems, Department of Transportation.
- **Academic Institutions**: High-traffic university corridors (e.g., NTU, NCKU).

## 🚀 2. Company Mission & Solution

> **"Converting Every Step into a Spark for Taiwan's Future."**

### Vision
To transform every urban surface into a generator, making citizens active contributors to the national energy pool.

### The Solution: Kinetic Tiles
1. **Electromagnetic Induction Units**: Modular floor tiles using high-efficiency induction to generate **2–5 Joules** per footfall. Peak output hits **7W**, optimized for high-traffic "choke points."
2. **Community Live Dashboard**: IoT-enabled screens that display "Total Carbon Offset" and "Community Steps" in real-time, gamifying public transit.
3. **Smart Storage**: Integrated super-capacitors that buffer energy during peak hours for emergency lighting or public smartphone charging ports.

## 📊 3. Business Model & Innovation

### Value Proposition
- **For Governments**: Low-cost public engagement tool for ESG reporting and sustainability education.
- **For Private Sector**: Ad-display visibility (Sogo/Shin-Kong Mitsukoshi) powered by the customer's own walking energy.

### 💡 Technological & Social Innovation
- **TENG Integration (Fase 2)**: Collaborating with Taiwanese academic labs (e.g., NCKU Hi-GEM) to integrate Triboelectric Nanogenerators (摩擦電奈米發電機) to harvest even minor vibrations.
- **EasyCard Rewards**: Mapping step-counts to tokens or EasyCard (悠遊卡) discount points, incentivizing carbon-neutral movement.

## 📈 5. Financial & Strategic Sustainability

### Strategic Roadmap
- **Year 1**: Deployment of 10-tile pilot at a Taipei MRT station (e.g., Zhongxiao Fuxing).
- **Year 2**: B2B expansion into department store entrances and large hotel lobbies.
- **Year 5**: Integration into 30% of major Taiwan transit nodes, targeting an aggregate annual offset equivalent to **5,000 houses.**
