## 1. Social Problem Analysis
Taiwan is under immense pressure for energy transition. Major urban centers (MRT stations, transit hubs, underground walkways) have millions of daily commuters whose "walking energy" is currently wasted as ambient heat/noise.
- **Core Challenge**: Lack of low-threshold solutions to convert "everyday behavior" into "green electricity."
- **Target Demographic**: Taipei MRT, major public transit hubs, and smart city infrastructure.

## 2. Company Mission & Solution
### Vision
To make every citizen's "step" a power source for the city's future.
### Solution & Product
1.  **Electromagnetic Kinetic Tiles**: Durable, modular tiles generating 5-8 watts per step.
2.  **Visual Data Monitoring**: Real-time dashboards showing "Total Carbon Saved" and "Live Generation."
3.  **Micro-Grid Integration**: Directing power to MRT signage, emergency exit lights, or phone charging stations.

## 3. Business Model
- **Value Proposition**: Offering public sectors a high-engagement demonstrator of smart city transition.
- **Revenue Streams**:
    - **B2G (Procurements)**: Smart city infrastructure installation fees.
    - **B2B (Leasing)**: Ad-wall power for malls (e.g., SOGO, Shin-Kong) to showcase CSR initiatives.
- **Cost Structure**: Molds for tiling, EM induction modules, and digital UI development.

## 4. Innovation & Uniqueness
- **Visible Sustainability**: Unlike rooftop solar panels, kinetic tiles are interactive, allowing citizens to participate in green energy generation directly.
- **Low-Maintenance Modular Design**: Individual tiles can be swapped without total floor overhaul.

## 5. Expected Social Impact
- **Energy Saving**: Estimated 10% emergency lighting supply in Taipei Main Station pilot.
- **Public Engagement**: Linking stepcounts to "EasyCard" reward points to encourage walking and carbon-neutral transit.

## 6. Financial Sustainability Planning
- **Initial Funding**: Seeking social enterprise venture capital and government "Smart City" grants.
- **Mid-to-Long Term**: Utilizing "Big Data" (foot-traffic heatmaps) to provide smart-store site selection consultancy.
- **5-Year Forecast**: Technical maturity and full ROI projected by Year 4.

## 7. Human Resources & Team
