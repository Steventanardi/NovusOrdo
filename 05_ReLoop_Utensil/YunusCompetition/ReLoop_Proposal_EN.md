### 1. Social Problem Analysis
**The "Plastic Paradox" in Taiwan/Kinmen**
Taiwan consumes 8 billion disposable containers annually. In Kinmen, the crisis is magnified because all waste must be shipped to Taiwan by boat, creating high carbon footprints and logistics costs. Grease-contaminated paper boxes are often non-recyclable in practice, leading to overflowing campus bins and public health issues at NQU.

---

### 2. Goals, Vision & Service Content
**Vision: "A campus where every box comes home, and starts again."**
We aim to become the first self-sustaining "No-Waste Campus System" in Kinmen by 2030. 
**Status**: Financial pilot complete for a 100-meal/day Phase 0, demonstrating a 107-day ROI.
