# 🏆 6th Taiwan Yunus Innovation Award - Competition Requirements
## 第六屆台灣尤努斯創新獎 — 參賽規範與指南

> **Important**: This document summarizes the official requirements for the 2026 competition. All submissions must follow these rules to pass the preliminary screening.

---

## 📅 Key Deadlines
*   **Registration & Submission**: May 31, 2026 (23:59)
*   **Preliminary Results**: July 29, 2026
*   **Final Materials Due**: September 2, 2026
*   **Final Pitch**: September 12, 2026

---

## 👥 Eligibility (Student Group / 學生組)
*   **Team Size**: 2–5 members.
*   **Age**: 18–30 years old.
*   **Status**: All members must be currently enrolled students (provide proof of enrollment).

---

## 📄 Proposal Formatting (MANDATORY)
*   **Language**: Traditional Chinese (Mandarin).
*   **Page Limit**: **Max 10 pages** (inclusive of cover, excluding appendices if allowed).
*   **Page Size**: A4, portrait orientation.
*   **Font**: **BiauKai (標楷體)**.
*   **Font Size**: 12pt.
*   **Line Spacing**: **1.5 lines**.
*   **File Format**: **PDF**.
*   **Cover Page**: Must clearly state Team Name, Project Name, and Members.

---

## 📊 Evaluation Criteria (Preliminary)
| Criterion | Weight | Key Focus |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Project Feasibility** | **35%** | Operations, logistics, and pilot results. |
| **Social Problem Analysis**| **20%** | Depth of understanding of Taiwan's waste crisis. |
| **Innovation** | **20%** | Uniqueness of the ReLoop model vs. competitors. |
| **Financial Planning** | **15%** | Revenue streams, CAPEX, and sustainability. |
| **Proposal Quality** | **10%** | Formatting, logic, and visual clarity. |

---

## 🏹 Required Proposal Content
1.  **Project Abstract**: Concise summary of the goal.
2.  **Social Problem**: Analysis of the single-use bento box crisis in Taiwan/Kinmen.
3.  **Social Business Solution**: Detailed ReLoop UaaS model description.
4.  **Target Market**: Students at NQU, canteen vendors, campus administration.
5.  **Social Impact**: Quantified waste reduction and employment of disadvantaged groups.
6.  **Financial Strategy**: 3-year revenue plan and Phase 0/1 CAPEX.
7.  **Execution Roadmap**: Pilot timeline and expansion plans.

---

## 💰 Prizes
*   **1st Place**: NT$100,000
*   **2nd Place**: NT$60,000
*   **3rd Place**: NT$30,000
*   **Bonus**: Carnegie Training Course for all student finalists (Value NT$35,000).
