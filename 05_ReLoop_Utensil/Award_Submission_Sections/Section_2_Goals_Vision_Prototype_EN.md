# Award Submission Section 2 (English)

## 2. Company Goals, Vision, and Product Content
**Novus Ordo Patera** is dedicated to redefining the university food experience by delivering a "Zero-Waste, Zero-Friction" ecosystem.

### A. Vision & Goals
*   **Vision**: To become the standard infrastructure for circular dining in Taiwan, making single-use plastic obsolete through economic superiority rather than just "environmental awareness."
*   **Core Goal (Social)**: To divert 1,000,000 pieces of single-use food packaging from incinerators within the first 3 years of operation.
*   **Core Goal (Economic)**: To prove that social businesses can cover 100% of their operational costs (including fair wages and market rent) solely through service fees, achieving total financial independence.

### B. Product & Service Content
Our primary service is the **Patera Loop**:
1.  **The Vessel**: High-durability, BPA-free, ISO-certified reusable food containers equipped with heat-resistant RFID tags.
2.  **The Patera Registry**: A digital backbone that tracks sanitization cycles and returns, providing users with absolute transparency regarding hygiene.
3.  **Smart Recovery Network**: A system of geo-located "Smart Bins" across campus hubs for effortless returns.

### C. Prototype & Initial Verification Results (Steven Tanardi)
We have progressed beyond the ideation stage into the **Operational Verification** phase:
1.  **The Digital Prototype**: We have completed a full **Financial Stress Test (Worst-Case Scenario)**, verifying that the business remains net-zero or profitable even if income drops to 0 during university holidays.
2.  **Economic Audit (Verification)**: Initial cost-comparison audits show that our service (NT$3.30–4.00/use) is **20%–33% cheaper** than the status quo of high-quality single-use bento boxes (NT$5.20/use). This verifies the "Economic Superiority" of the model.
3.  **Operational Pilot (Phase 0)**: A roadmap for a 100-meal/day pilot at **National Quemoy University (NQU)** has been finalized, identifying the necessary low-risk CAPEX (NT$68,000) for entry.
4.  **The "Anti-Fragile" Proof**: Our **Deposit System (NT$150 Deposit vs NT$120 Cost)** has been verified as a risk-mitigation tool that turns inventory loss into a revenue event, solving the primary barrier to circular scaling.
