## 🛡️ Asset Management & Quality Control (Small Scale)

1.  **"Honor System" Collection**: 
    Students scan a QR code on the box before dropping it into the bin. The LINE bot sends a "Thank You" and updates their **Carbon Rank**.
2.  **FIFO Inventory**: 
    The washing team logs batches on a shared **LINE Keep** note to ensure no container sits for more than 48 hours without use.
3.  **Circular End-of-Life**: 
    Damaged containers are collected and sent to the **BSF Bio-Loop** project to be ground into feed trays, ensuring zero waste.
