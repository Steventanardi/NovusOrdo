## 馃嚞馃嚙 English Version

### 1. The Reality Check: Will there be a surplus every day or month?
**To tell the truth:** No, there will **NOT** be a surplus every day in the beginning. 
- **The Deficit Phase (Months 1-3):** When you launch, you will likely process fewer than 337 containers per day. During this phase, you will suffer a **daily deficit (loss)** because your daily fixed costs (NT$ 733) and labor outpace your revenue. 
- **The Surplus Phase:** You will only start seeing a daily surplus *after* you consistently process **more than 337 containers per day**.
- **The Cash Flow Reality:** Even when you achieve a daily operational surplus, your *cumulative* cash flow will remain negative for about 18 to 24 months due to the initial capital investment (CAPEX) of NT$ 1,930,000 needed to start the business.

## 馃嚠馃嚛 Indonesian Version (Versi Bahasa Indonesia)

### 1. Pengecekan Realita: Apakah akan ada surplus setiap hari atau bulan?
**Sejujurnya:** Tidak, **TIDAK** akan ada surplus setiap hari pada tahap awal.
- **Fase Defisit (Bulan 1-3):** Saat pertama kali meluncurkan bisnis, Anda kemungkinan akan memproses kurang dari 337 wadah per hari. Selama fase ini, Anda akan mengalami **defisit harian (kerugian)** karena biaya tetap harian (NT$ 733) dan upah tenaga kerja lebih besar daripada pendapatan Anda.
- **Fase Surplus:** Anda baru akan mulai melihat surplus harian *setelah* Anda secara konsisten memproses **lebih dari 337 wadah per hari**.
- **Realita Arus Kas (Cash Flow):** Bahkan ketika Anda sudah mencapai surplus harian, arus kas *kumulatif* Anda akan tetap negatif selama sekitar 18 hingga 24 bulan karena besarnya investasi modal awal (CAPEX) sebesar NT$ 1,930,000 yang dibutuhkan.
