# 🍱 ReLoop: Reusable Utensil-as-a-Service (UaaS)
**The "YouBike for Bento" Circular Economy Solution for NQU**

---

## 1. Executive Summary
ReLoop is a closed-loop infrastructure system designed to eliminate single-use bento boxes on university campuses. By providing high-quality, airtight CPET containers and a distributed "Scan-and-Drop" return network, we provide a more convenient and sustainable alternative to disposable packaging.

### Core Value Proposition:
*   **For Vendors**: Savings of 20-33% on packaging costs (NT$5.2 -> NT$4).
*   **For Students**: Premium dining experience with leak-proof, high-temp safe containers.
*   **For University**: 30,000+ units of waste reduced annually per 100-serving hub.

## 2. Operational Workflow
Our system relies on the **"Collect-Sanitize-Deploy"** cycle, managed via a LINE Bot for transparency.

### A. The User Journey
1.  **Checkout**: Student pays a NT$150 deposit (via LINE Pay) and receives their food in a ReLoop box.
2.  **Dining**: Airtight seal keeps food fresh and prevents leaks in backpacks.
3.  **Return**: Student scans the QR code on any **Eco-Bin** return station and drops the box inside.
4.  **Refund**: The system automatically releases the NT$150 deposit back to the student's account.

### B. The Logistics Cycle
1.  **Collection**: Staff collects boxes from Eco-Bins twice daily.
2.  **Sanitization**: Use of industrial high-temp dishwashers (80°C+) to ensure 99.9% sterility.
3.  **Quality Control**: Visual inspection for asset condition before redistribution.

## 4. Resilience & Risk Management (Stress Test)
What happens if the "Worst Case" occurs?

*   **Hypothesis**: High asset loss (5% daily) will kill the business.
*   **Pivot**: The **NT$150 Deposit System**. If a box is lost, the student pays $150. Since the box costs us $120, a "Lost Box" actually generates a **NT$30 profit**.
*   **Result**: The business is **financially unkillable** because every lost asset is a completed sale at 25% margin.
