# 🏦 ReLoop: Pure Commercial Market Analysis & Stress Test

## 1. Executive Summary: The Commercial Reality
This report analyzes ReLoop as a **standalone commercial entity** at NQU. We assume zero university subsidies, 100% paid market rent, and full professional costs.

---

## 2. Fixed Cost Structure (The "Landlord" Burden)
To operate a washing hub at NQU as a private tenant:
*   **Space Rent**: NT$8,000 / month (Small utility plot in canteen corner).
*   **Utility Overhead (Static)**: NT$2,000 / month (Minimum water/electricity meter charge).
*   **Insurance & Licensing**: NT$1,500 / month.
*   **TOTAL MONTHLY FIXED: NT$11,500**.
*   **DAILY FIXED COST (30 days): NT$383 / day.**

---

## 3. Variable Cost Structure (Per 100 Servings/Day)
*   **Labor (2 hrs @ NT$196/hr)**: NT$392.
*   **Consumables (Eco-Soap/Heat)**: NT$80.
*   **Asset Loss (2% @ $120 cost)**: NT$240 (2 boxes lost every 100 meals).
*   **TOTAL VARIABLE COST: NT$712 / day.**

### **TOTAL DAILY OPERATING COST: NT$1,095 / day.**

---

## 4. Revenue Generation Scenarios

### Scenario A: The "Vendor Only" Failure
*   Revenue: 100 meals * NT$4 = $400.
*   **Net Result: -NT$695 / day.**
*   *Verdict*: Commercially impossible.

### Scenario B: The "Subscription Break-Even" (The Pilot Fix)
To cover $1,095 total cost, we must earn **NT$10.95 per box**.
*   Vendor pays: $4.00.
*   **Student pays: NT$7.00 Service Fee.**
*   **Net Result: NT$5 / day profit.**
*   *Verdict*: Financially stable but low margin.

### Scenario C: The "Scale Victory" (1,000 Servings/Day)
At 1,000 servings, fixed costs are shared across more units:
*   Daily Fixed: $600 (larger space).
*   Daily Variable: $980 (Labor) + $2,400 (Loss) + $500 (Soap).
*   Total Daily Cost: **NT$4,480**.
*   ---
*   **Total Revenue** ($4 Vendor + $10 Surcharge): **NT$14,000**.
*   **DAILY PROFIT: NT$9,520**.
*   *Verdict*: **High-Value Social Enterprise.**

---

## 5. Critical Hypotheses (Pure Commercial Model)

1.  **Hypothesis: Scaling is Mandatory.**
    Our break-even point is **274 boxes/day**. Below this number, the business loses money every day. Above this number, every box is pure profit.
2.  **Hypothesis: Deposit is non-negotiable.**
    In a commercial model, we cannot afford to lose boxes. A **NT$150 deposit** is the only way to protect the initial $68,000 CAPEX.
3.  **Hypothesis: Price Resistance.**
    Will students pay $7 per meal? We believe yes, because a **plastic bag and 5 minutes of extra trash disposal time** is worth more than $7 to a busy NQU student.

---

## 6. Project Roadmap for Judges
"We enter the market at a loss of **NT$695/day** (Phase 0) to prove the system works. Once we secure 300+ daily servings, we hit the **Break-Even Plateau**. At 1,000 servings, ReLoop has the cash flow to expand to the entire Kinmen county."
