# 🍱 ReLoop vs. Disposable: Canteen Economic Analysis
**Objective:** To demonstrate the financial and operational superiority of ReLoop over traditional single-use packaging.

## 1. Executive Summary: The $5 Rule
Based on real-world canteen experience, the average cost of high-quality, leak-proof single-use bento packaging is **NT$4–6 per serving**. This represents a 100% sunk cost that ends up in a landfill or incinerator within minutes.

ReLoop converts this waste into a circular service provided at **NT$4 per serving**, delivering an immediate **20%–33% reduction** in direct costs for the canteen.

## 3. Operational Workflow Comparison

### A. The "Disposables" Nightmare (Current)
1.  **Inventory Bloat**: Canteen must store 1 month's supply (30,000 units) due to delivery fluctuations.
2.  **Manual Prep**: Staff spend 1–2 hours daily folding and stacking paper boxes.
3.  **Trash Logistics**: Staff must manage "Bento Clogging" in bins—overflowing trash that attracts pests and causes bad odors.
4.  **Financial Loss**: 100% of the NT$5.00 spent is "thrown away" every single time a student eats.

### B. The "ReLoop" Seamless Flow
1.  **Just-In-Time Delivery**: ReLoop delivers clean, nested stacks daily. No storage needed.
2.  **Premium Experience**: ReLoop (Airtight CPET) is 100% leak-proof, increasing student satisfaction.
3.  **Distributed Return**: Students return to **Eco-Bins** across campus. Canteen staff NEVER touch dirty laundry.
4.  **Asset Security**: If a student keeps the box, the **Deposit System** covers the inventory cost. The canteen has **Zero Financial Risk** for lost assets.
