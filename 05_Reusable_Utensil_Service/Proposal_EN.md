## 1. Social Problem Analysis
Despite plastic-restriction policies, Taiwan's takeout culture consumes hundreds of millions of single-use boxes and utensils annually. Many "eco-friendly" containers are still resin-lined and hard to recycle.
- **Core Challenge**: Consumers want convenience; restaurants don't want the labor cost of dishwashing.
- **Target Demographic**: SME bento shops, hand-shaken drink chains, and corporate buildings.

## 2. Company Mission & Solution
### Vision
To provide a "Utensil-as-a-Service" model for Taiwan's takeout culture.
### Solution & Product
1.  **Subscription Rental (B2B)**: Providing stainless steel (316) and high-strength eco-polymer containers.
2.  **Standardized Sanitization Centers**: ISO 22000 and HACCP-level high-temperature cleaning hubs.
3.  **Reverse Logistics**: Scheduled daily circulation to deliver clean utensils and pick up used items.

## 3. Business Model
- **Subscription Fee**: Monthly fee to restaurants, lower than dishwasher labor and single-use box procurement.
- **Deposit System (B2C)**: Integrated App for scanning utensils at checkout and returning at any partner point.
- **Data Analysis**: Providing restaurants with "Waste Reduction Reports" for ESG certification.

## 4. Innovation & Uniqueness
- **Reverse-Logistics Optimization**: Specialized route mapping and cleaning-detergent efficiency compared to general logistics.
- **RFID Tracking**: Micro-tags in every set for inventory tracking and "total lifecycle" monitoring.

## 5. Expected Social Impact
- **Massive Reduction**: Targeting 500,000 fewer single-use boxes in the first full year.
- **Inclusive Employment**: Providing diverse local and disabled job opportunities at sanitization and logistics hubs.

## 6. Financial Sustainability Planning
- **Profit Logic**: Economies of scale achieved after 100 partner restaurants.
- **5-Year Forecast**: Full-profit target by Year 5 with automated cleaning tech and expansion into the hand-shaken drink market.

## 7. Human Resources & Team
- **Project Lead / Steven Tanardi**: Strategy development, food-safety compliance, and business model logic design.
- **Operations & Logistics Manager**: Managing efficient regional route mapping.
- **Quality Assurance**: Monitoring microbial residues to ensure medical-grade hygiene.
