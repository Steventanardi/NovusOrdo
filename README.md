## 🔬 Core Initiative Overviews

### 🍎 1. Upcycled Produce ("Ugly" Transformation)
A logistics network that buys aesthetically rejected fruits from Taiwanese farmers and converts them into high-value dehydrated snacks and fermented products.
- **Goal**: Zero food waste & higher farmer income.

### ⚡ 2. Kinetic Energy Harvesting (School Scale)
A 10-tile pilot designed for dormitory and school corridors. Converts urban motion into real-time public energy dashboards and night-light power.
- **Goal**: Community-driven renewable micro-grids.

### 🔎 3. AI-Vision Smart Trash Scanner
A multi-sensor (NIR + RGB) system built to solve Taiwan's complex 13-bin sorting challenge with 99% precision.
- **Goal**: Molecular-purity recycling streams.

### 🐛 4. Black Soldier Fly (BSF) Composting
The world's most efficient organic waste converter. Diverting night market food scraps into premium larvae protein (Fish feed) and frass (Fertilizer).
- **Goal**: 100% biological waste-to-feed loop.

### 🍴 5. Reusable Utensil Subscription
A monthly B2B service for restaurants to eliminate single-use plastics. Focuses on medical-grade centralized sanitation and scheduled logistics.
- **Goal**: Zero plastic takeout culture.

### 🧪 6. Algae-Oxygen Vertical Wall
Indoor vertical bioreactors for Taipei's high-density apartments. Uses algae to produce 10x more oxygen than houseplants while scrubbing urban PM2.5.
- **Goal**: Living urban bio-filters.
