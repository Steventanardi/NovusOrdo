## 🔬 Core Initiative Overviews

### 🍎 1. Upcycled Produce ("Ugly" Transformation)
A logistics network that buys aesthetically rejected fruits from Taiwanese farmers and converts them into high-value dehydrated snacks and fermented products using vacuum-drying tech.
- **Goal**: Zero food waste & 25% higher farmer net profit.

### ⚡ 2. Kinetic Energy Harvesting (Grid Scale)
Smart flooring modules designed for high-traffic Taiwan transit hubs. Converts urban motion into renewable micro-grids and interactive public education dashboards.
- **Goal**: Community-driven renewable micro-prowess.

### 🔎 3. Molecular-Precision AI Sorting
A multimodal (NIR + 4K RGB) system built to achieve 99.5% purity in recycling streams, identifying resin types at the chemical level.
- **Goal**: True circular polymer loops.

### 🐛 4. BSF Bio-Loop (Insect Transformation)
The most efficient organic waste converter. Diverting night market food scraps into premium larvae protein (Fish feed) and frass (Organic fertilizer).
- **Goal**: 100% biological waste-to-feed autonomy.

### 🍴 5. The Circular Dish (Reuse Logistics)
A B2B subscription service for restaurants to eliminate single-use plastics. Provides ISO 22000-grade sanitation and automated RFID-tracked reverse logistics.
- **Goal**: Erasure of the single-use plastic takeout culture.

### 🧪 6. Algae-Oxygen Vertical Wall (Bio-Reactor)
Indoor vertical bioreactors for Taipei's high-density apartments. Uses *Chlorella* to produce 10x more oxygen than houseplants while scrubbing PM2.5.
- **Goal**: Decentralized living urban bio-filtration.
