## 1. Social Problem Analysis
Taiwan's vibrant night market culture generates immense volumes of solid food waste. This waste is high in salt and oil, making traditional composting difficult and leading to dioxin risks during incineration.
- **Core Challenge**: Inefficient and high-cost processing of night market swill.
- **Target Demographic**: Shilin, Raohe, and Luodong Night Market associations, agriculture bureaus, and livestock farmers.

## 2. Company Mission & Solution
### Vision
To utilize biological instincts to put "waste" back into the food chain within 24 hours.
### Solution & Product
1.  **Black Soldier Fly (BSF) Larvae Processing**: 24/7 modular hubs converting food waste into high-protein biomass (40% crude protein).
2.  **Whole-Insect Protein Feed**: Producing fishmeal alternatives for Taiwan's aquaculture and poultry farms.
3.  **BSF Frass Fertilizer**: High-efficiency, odorless organic fertilizer for urban farming.

## 3. Business Model
- **Tipping Fees**: Charging night markets and restaurants a waste processing fee lower than traditional disposal.
- **Feed Sales**: Selling dried larvae to Tilapia and Shrimp farmers in Central/Southern Taiwan.
- **Bio-Soil Sales**: Packaging frass as "Black Gold Soil" for flower market retail.

## 4. Innovation & Uniqueness
- **Zero-Emission Nature**: Unlike traditional landfills producing methane, BSF generates almost no GHG.
- **Automated Bio-Container**: Mobile units placed near major markets to process waste onsite, reducing transport carbon footprint.

## 5. Expected Social Impact
- **Waste Reduction**: Targeting over 500 tons of night market food waste reduction annually.
- **Agricultural Sustainability**: Substituting 20% of imported soy/fishmeal with local protein.

## 6. Financial Sustainability Planning
- **Profit Logic**: Dual-revenue model (Front-end tipping + Back-end sales).
- **5-Year Forecast**: Scale profitability within 3 years due to the rapid 14-20 day insect growth cycle and high turnover.

## 7. Human Resources & Team
- **Project Lead / Steven Tanardi**: Strategy development, bio-module efficiency, and livestock sales coordination.
- **Insect Pathologists**: Managing fly breeding and health.
- **Circular Supply Chain Managers**: Logistics for night market collection points.
