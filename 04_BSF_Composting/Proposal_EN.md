## 🏛️ 1. Social Problem Analysis

### ⚠️ The "Night Market Swill" Crisis
Taiwan's iconic night markets (Shilin, Raohe, Fengjia) generate massive volumes of organic waste. However, this swill (廚餘) is notoriously difficult to process due to:
- **High Lipid/Salt content**, which kills traditional composting bacteria.
- **Infrastructural Strain**: Traditional landfills are reaching 95%+ capacity, leading to dioxin risks during incineration of wet waste.

- **The Problem**: Over **1,000 tons** of food waste is generated daily from Taiwan's commercial sectors. 
- **The Gap**: Most "Bio-Waste" solutions are centralized, requiring carbon-intensive transport over long distances to rural composting plants.

### 🎯 Target Demographic
- **City Market Associations**: Shilin, Ningxia, and Luodong associations.
- **Aquaculture Farmers**: Tilapia and shrimp producers needing local, sustainable feed.

## 🚀 2. Company Mission & Solution

> **"Returning Organic Waste to the Food Chain within 24 Hours."**

### Vision
To substitute 20% of Taiwan's imported fishmeal with locally-engineered BSF protein by 2030, building biological resilience.

### The Solution: Bio-Modular Hubs
1. **Black Soldier Fly (BSF) Larvae Reactor**: 24/7 modular aerobic hubs using BSF larvae (Hermetia illucens) to reduce waste mass by **70% in under 12 days**.
2. **Whole-Insect Protein Feed**: Turning dried larvae into 40%-high-quality crude protein pellets for Taiwan's poultry and aquaculture industries.
3. **BSF Frass (Organic Fertilizer)**: A slow-release, odorless and pathogen-free fertilizer derived from larvae excrement—perfect for Taiwan's urban farming movement.

## 📊 3. Business Model & Innovation

### Value Proposition
- **For Governments**: Lower tipping fees and 0% methane waste processing compared to traditional methods.
- **For Farmers**: 15-20% lower feed costs with improved animal immune response via natural antimicrobial peptides in larvae.

### 💡 Technological & Social Innovation
- **Decentralized "Bio-Containers"**: Shipping-container-sized reactors placed in proximity to major night markets, neutralizing waste at the source.
- **Precision IoT Feeding**: Sensors to monitor larval health, moisture, and temperature, ensuring maximum conversion efficiency.
