# Yunus Innovation Award - Implementation Summary (EN/ID/ZH)

## 🌟 Vision / 願景 / Visi
To create applicable social business seeds in Kinmen that solve local environmental issues while creating dignified employment.

---

### [EN] Realistic Implementation Strategy
- **Not "Too Good to be True"**: We acknowledge that biological systems (BSF) and human behavior (Returning cups) are unpredictable. Our plans include **Mitigation Buffers** (Replacement stock, sensor monitoring).
- **Phased Growth**: We do not propose an island-wide rollout immediately. We start with **Pilot Blocks** to stabilize the business model first.
- **Financial Honesty**: Initial phases focus on **Self-Sufficiency** aided by award prize money, rather than immediate massive profits.

---

### [ID] Strategi Implementasi Realistis
- **Bukan Janji Manis**: Kami mengakui bahwa sistem biologi (BSF) dan perilaku manusia (Mengembalikan gelas) tidak dapat diprediksi. Rencana kami mencakup **Buffer Mitigasi** (Stok pengganti, pemantauan sensor).
- **Pertumbuhan Bertahap**: Kami tidak mengusulkan peluncuran seluruh pulau segera. Kami memulai dengan **Blok Pilot** untuk menstabilkan model bisnis terlebih dahulu.
- **Kejujuran Finansial**: Tahap awal fokus pada **Kemandirian** yang dibantu oleh uang hadiah penghargaan, bukan keuntungan besar instan.

---

### [ZH] 務實的實施策略
- **拒絕空談**：我們認可生物系統（黑水虻）與人類行為（歸還容器）具備不可預測性。企劃中包含**風險緩衝機制**（耗損備品、感測器監測）。
- **階段性成長**：我們不建議立即全島推行，而是從**示範街區**開始穩定商業模式。
- **財務誠實**：初期重點在於透過獎金補助達到**自給自足**，而非立即獲取暴利。
