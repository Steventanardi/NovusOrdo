# Kin-Loop: Tourist Reusable Container Network
### Reducing Island Waste at Source in Kinmen

## 1. Social Issue (Practical Context)
As a remote island, Kinmen has zero incineration waste facilities. All tourist-generated plastic and paper cups must be shipped back to Taiwan for processing—a high-carbon, high-cost endeavor. Tourists in Jincheng Houpu Street often create unsightly litter near historical sites.

## 2. Practical Solution
A localized **Circular Cup Library**. Instead of building a complex app-heavy system, we use a simple **LINE-based rental system** focused on the highest-density tourist block (Houpu). We start with 5 core shops to prove the logistics of washing and retrieval.

## 3. Operational Workflow (Step-by-Step)
1. **The Deposit**: Customers scan a QR code at the drink shop, pay a small deposit (or link a credit card), and receive a high-quality reusable cup.
2. **Mobility**: Customers walk through the old town, finishing their drink.
3. **The Return**: Cups are dropped into "Smart Return Bins" located at 3 strategic exit points of the street.
4. **Logistics & Cleaning**: Twice a day, an E-scooter collects the dirty cups and brings them to a **Social Washing Station**.
5. **Quality Control**: Each cup is inspected for damage and sanitized using medical-grade UV and high-temp heat.

## 4. Key Implementation Steps (The "How-To")
- **Phase A (Merchant Onboarding)**: Negotiate a "Lower-than-Plastic-Cup" price for shops (e.g., 2 NTD service fee vs 2.5 NTD for a premium eco-plastic cup).
- **Phase B (Small Batch Testing)**: Launch with 200 cups to test the "Loss Rate." *Realism check: We expect a 5-10% loss rate due to tourists taking cups as souvenirs.*
- **Phase C (Impact Branding)**: Design the cup as a "Badge of Honor" for eco-tourists.

## 5. Risks & Mitigation (Realism Check)
- **Risk**: Hygiene concerns from customers.
- **Mitigation**: Transparent washing station with glass windows so public can see the cleaning process.
- **Risk**: High loss rate (The "Souvenir Problem").
- **Mitigation**: Sell a "Special Edition" souvenir cup separately to satisfy the desire for souvenirs without depleting the rental stock.

## 6. Business & Social Impact
- **Profitability**: Revenue through shop service fees and branding stickers on cups.
- **Yunus Alignment**: Employs disadvantaged local staff for cleaning and logistics, ensuring the "Third Zero" (Zero Unemployment).
