# 🔬 Research Supplement: Maximizing Cup Return Rates
## Behavioral Engineering & Incentive Design for KinLoop

### Quick Summary of "Push-Pull" Strategies
To ensure tourists return their cups in Kinmen, we must move beyond simple deposits.

#### 1. The "Ubike" convenience
*   **Return Anywhere**: Cups must be returnable at any partner shop or smart bin.
*   **Last-Mile Bins**: High-visibility bins at the **Airport** and **Ferry Terminal** are crucial to catch departing tourists.

#### 2. Behavioral "Green Nudges"
*   **Gamification**: Bins with "Basketball Hoops" or counters showing "Cups Saved Today."
*   **Social Proof**: Make the cup a "Badge of Honor" for eco-tourists via design.

#### 3. Economic Incentives
*   **Loss Aversion**: A 30 NTD deposit is felt more strongly than a discount.
*   **Loop Vouchers**: Reward returns with stamps on LINE towards a local snack or gift.

#### 4. The "Souvenir Problem"
*   **Upgrade Fee**: Allow tourists to "buy" the cup if they love it, but keep the rental price low.
*   **Official Souvenirs**: Sell a premium version separately so rentals stay in circulation.

---
> [!TIP]
> **Strategic Recommendation**: Place a "KinLoop Gratitude Station" at the security gate of the airport. It captures the 10% of inventory usually lost to forgetful departing travelers.
