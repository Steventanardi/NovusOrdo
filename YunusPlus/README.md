# 6th Taiwan Yunus Innovation Award - Proposed Ideas

Welcome to the project directory for the 6th Taiwan Yunus Innovation Award. This proposed suite of social business ideas is localized for **Taiwan** with a specific focus on **Kinmen (金門)**.

## 💡 Proposed Ideas

| Idea name | Social Focus | Scale | Location |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Brew-to-Bloom** | Circular Economy / Agriculture | Pilot (Village-scale) | Kinmen (Distillery areas) |
| **Kin-Loop** | Waste Reduction / Tourism | District (Street-scale) | Kinmen (Jincheng/Tourist hubs) |

---

## 🎯 Alignment with Yunus "Three Zeros"
- **Zero Net Carbon Emissions**: Localizing production and waste management.
- **Zero Wealth Concentration**: Profits reinvested in local community employment.
- **Zero Unemployment**: Creating technical and operational roles for local youth and seniors.
