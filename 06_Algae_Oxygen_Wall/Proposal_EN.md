## 1. Social Problem Analysis
Basin cities like Taipei and Taichung often face PM2.5 accumulation. Urbanites spend 90% of their time indoors, facing "Sick Building Syndrome" (high CO2 and VOCs) which leads to health issues and reduced focus.
- **Core Challenge**: Standard air purifiers only filter particles; they do not produce oxygen and rely on disposable filters.
- **Target Demographic**: Small Taipei apartment owners, co-working spaces, nursing centers, and eco-friendly real estate projects.

## 2. Company Mission & Solution
### Vision
To micro-scale a "Living Amazon Forest" onto every urban apartment wall.
### Solution & Product
1.  **Micro-Algae Bioreactor**: Slim, transparent vertical panels containing live *Chlorella* (Algae) solution.
2.  **Active Air Induction**: Silent fans pull indoor air into the reactor, where algae photosynthesis absorbs CO2 and emits pure O2.
3.  **IoT Nutrient Maintenance**: Automated monitoring of algae density and trace element supplementation.

## 3. Business Model
- **Product Sales (B2C)**: Kits sold via high-end interior and sustainable design contractors.
- **Nutrient Subscription**: Monthly delivery of "Algae Growth Pods" and filter sponges.
- **Biological Carbon Sequestration (B2B)**: Providing office buildings with carbon-offset data for CSR reports.

## 4. Innovation & Uniqueness
- **10x Efficiency**: 1 square meter of bio-wall produces as much oxygen as 10 mature trees.
- **Bio-Aesthetic Glow**: Providing active bio-lighting (calming green glow) that improves mental well-being.

## 5. Expected Social Impact
- **Respiratory Health**: Dramatically reducing indoor CO2 levels (under 800ppm), improving long-term health.
- **Zero-Waste Cycle**: Dead algae can be used as liquid fertilizer for houseplants, achieving a 100% biological circular loop.

## 6. Financial Sustainability Planning
- **Profit Logic**: High-margin functional furniture category; targeting residential hubs first, then moving to medical/nursing care facilities.
- **5-Year Forecast**: Technical maturity and profit expected by Year 4.

## 7. Human Resources & Team
- **Project Lead / Steven Tanardi**: Strategy development, algae parameter database management, and sustainable interior market sales.
- **Biochemistry Researchers**: Focusing on algae strain selection and growth efficiency.
- **Industrial Designers**: Managing bioreactor leak-proofing and aesthetic integration.
