## 🏛️ 1. Social Problem Analysis

### ⚠️ The "Sick Building" Syndrome in Taipei
In basin cities like Taipei and Taichung, poor air circulation leads to the accumulation of PM2.5, volatile organic compounds (VOCs), and excess CO2 indoors.
- **The Core Problem**: Urbanites spend **90% of their time indoors**. High CO2 levels (>1,000 ppm) cause drowsiness, respiratory irritation, and long-term cognitive decline.
- **The Tech Gap**: Standard HEPA air purifiers filter particles but **do not regenerate oxygen**. They rely on disposable, non-biodegradable filters.
- **The Environmental Cost**: Cities lack the vertical space to plant enough mature trees to offset indoor carbon intensity.

### 🎯 Target Demographic
- **Small Apartment Owners**: Taipei citizens living in compact units (<20 pings).
- **Medical & Senior Care**: Nursing centers and clinics seeking improved respiratory air quality.
- **Sustainable Real Estate**: Developers aiming for LEED or WELL building certifications.

## 🚀 2. Company Mission & Solution

> **"Micro-Scaling an Amazon Rainforest onto Every Urban Wall."**

### Vision
To provide **Active Bio-Oxygen** as a subscription-based utility, ending the reliance on stagnant, mechanical air filtration.

### The Solution: The Bio-Wall
1. **Vertical Micro-Algae Bioreactor**: High-transmission acrylic panels containing a specialized *Chlorella* solution.
2. **Active Induction Photosynthesis**: Silent intake fans pull indoor CO2 into the solution. Under optimized LED spectra, the algae convert it into **Medical-Grade O2** with **10x the efficiency** of traditional houseplants.
3. **Biological PM2.5 Scrubbers**: The liquid medium acts as a natural dampener for airborne particles, trapping them in the biomass without the need for periodic filter replacement.

## 📊 3. Business Model & Innovation

### Value Proposition
- **For Residential Users**: Better sleep, higher focus, and a sleek, bio-aesthetic green glow (Bio-Lighting).
- **For B2B Offices**: Carbon-offset data derived from every liter of algae, supporting CSR and ESG goals (Biological Carbon Sequestration).

### 💡 Technological & Social Innovation
- **IoT Nutrient Delivery**: Automated sensors that monitor algae density and autonomously deliver nutrients to prevent solution "crashes."
- **Nol-Waste Circular Loop**: Every 6 months, the "spent" harvested algae can be used as premium liquid fertilizer for the user's other houseplants.
