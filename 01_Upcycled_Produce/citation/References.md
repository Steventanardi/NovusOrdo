### 📄 Primary Reference
- **Title**: *Report of Food Waste in Chinese Taipei (APEC Research)*
- **Source**: National Taiwan University (NTU) / APEC Food System
- **URL**: [https://apec-flows.ntu.edu.tw/](https://apec-flows.ntu.edu.tw/upload/edit/file/2%20SR_2016_C_S3-09_Prof.%20Tony%20Shih-Hsun%20Hsu.pdf)
- **Key Insight**: Detailed breakdown of post-harvest fruit/vegetable waste in Taipei market hubs, identifying a 20-30% loss rate due to aesthetic grading.

### 📝 Strategic Contribution
This paper provides the data foundation for the fiscal impact of upcycling "ugly" produce in the Yunus Innovation Award proposal, specifically the target volume for reduction in the 5-year forecast.
