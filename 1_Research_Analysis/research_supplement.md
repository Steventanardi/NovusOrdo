## 3. How to Improve Your "Research Proposal" Section
To make your proposal stand out, use these "Academic Keywords":
1. **Just Transition**: Ensuring that as we move to Net-Zero, workers and poor communities aren't left behind.
2. **ESG (Environmental, Social, and Governance)**: Connect your social business to how it helps corporations fulfill their ESG goals.
3. **Circular Economy**: Moving from "Take-Make-Waste" to a closed-loop system (especially relevant for water and plastic).
4. **Social Impact Assessment (SIA)**: Mention how you will *measure* your success (e.g., tons of water saved, number of elderly people assisted).
